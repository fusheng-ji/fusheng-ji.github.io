Short Bio
Wenbo Ji is a M.S. student in computer science at CAD Research Center, Tongji University, where he works on computer vision and machine learning.
He received a B.S. in Information and Computer Science from Department of Mathematics, Nanjing Tech University in 2021, where he was worked on Medical Image Processing.
He received National Encouragement Scholarship and The First Prize Scholarships in 2017-2021 for many times.
He was born on April 20th, 2000 in Huaian, China.
Research interests
I am working at the intersection between Machine learning, Architecture and Computer Vision, developing new machine learning methods to resolve the challenging problems in architecture and 3D Vision, especially focus on BIM (Building Information Model).

My long-term goal is to improve the architecture system, benefiting society directly by improving people's living environment.
